# Hyder Law, PC — Design Brainstorm

## Ground-Truth Reference

The user has explicitly requested replication of the reference site: https://di-salvo-law.vercel.app/

Fidelity to the reference OVERRIDES any alternative design direction. The design language is:

- **Background**: Deep charcoal / near-black (#0a0a0a to #111111)
- **Primary accent**: Gold (#C9A84C) — thin underline accents, eyebrow labels, stat numbers, buttons, hover states
- **Headline font**: Serif (Playfair Display)
- **Body font**: Sans-serif (Inter)
- **Eyebrow labels**: Small uppercase gold letter-spaced labels above section headings
- **Buttons**: Solid gold fill with dark text + outlined/ghost variant
- **Nav**: Transparent over hero → solid dark on scroll
- **Cards**: Dark cards with subtle borders, large numbered index (01, 02, 03...) in gold
- **Stat blocks**: Oversized gold numbers with small uppercase captions
- **Footer**: Multi-column dark footer with legal disclaimer bar
- **Animations**: Framer Motion scroll-triggered fade-up reveals

## Design Movement
Editorial Legal — a refined, authoritative aesthetic combining classical serif typography with modern dark UI.

## Core Principles
1. **Authority through restraint** — gold is used sparingly as an accent, never as a flood
2. **Directness** — the phone number and CTA are always visible, reflecting the attorney's "direct access" philosophy
3. **Editorial rhythm** — sections alternate between full-width bands and contained grids, creating a magazine-like reading flow
4. **Tactile depth** — subtle borders, gradients, and shadows give dark cards dimension without breaking the dark theme

## Color Philosophy
- Background: #0a0a0a (primary), #111111 (cards), #161616 (elevated surfaces)
- Gold accent: #C9A84C (primary), #D4B85E (hover/lighter), #B8963A (darker)
- Text: #E8E8E8 (headlines), #A0A0A0 (body), #707070 (muted)
- Borders: rgba(255,255,255,0.08) — subtle, never stark white

## Layout Paradigm
Full-width sections with contained content (max-w-6xl). Hero is full-height. Specialty cards use a numbered grid. Stat blocks are oversized and centered. Footer is multi-column.

## Signature Elements
1. Gold underline accent beneath every H1 and section heading
2. Numbered specialty cards (01–06) with oversized gold numerals
3. Eyebrow labels in gold uppercase letter-spaced text

## Typography System
- H1/H2: Playfair Display, 400–700 weight, serif
- Body: Inter, 300–600 weight, sans-serif
- Eyebrow labels: Inter, 600 weight, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.15em, gold

## Brand Essence
Hyder Law, PC — direct, experienced legal representation for Monroe County and Southeast Michigan. Personal, accessible, aggressive in the courtroom.

## Signature Brand Color
#C9A84C — a warm, dignified gold that conveys both authority and approachability.
